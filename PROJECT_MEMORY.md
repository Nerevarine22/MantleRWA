# Mantle RWA Intelligence Hub Knowledge Base

This file stores the current product knowledge base for the project.
It is a living document and should evolve as the product concept becomes more
specific over time.

## Product Vision

Mantle RWA Intelligence Hub is an AI-powered platform that helps users
explore, understand, analyze, and optimize investment opportunities across the
Real World Asset ecosystem on Mantle.

The platform combines market data, educational content, portfolio planning,
risk analysis, and yield discovery into a single user experience.

Rather than acting as a trading platform, the product serves as an intelligent
investment research and decision-support hub.

## Main User Experience

When a user enters the platform, they immediately gain access to four core
areas:

1. RWA Market Overview
2. AI Investment Copilot
3. RWA Academy
4. Yield Opportunities

The platform is designed to help users move from learning to research to
planning to optimization.

## 1. RWA Market Dashboard

The homepage displays real-time information about the RWA market.

Assets are grouped by categories:

### Tokenized Treasuries

- USDY
- OUSG
- Treasury products

### Tokenized Equities

- xStocks
- Tokenized ETFs

### Private Credit

- Maple
- Credit markets

### Real Estate

- Tokenized real estate assets

### Commodities

- Gold
- Commodity-backed assets

For each category, the platform should eventually display:

- Current price
- 24h change
- 7d change
- Market cap
- TVL
- Risk level

Users should be able to quickly understand which sectors are growing and where
capital is flowing.

## 2. AI Investment Copilot

This is the core platform feature.

The AI assistant should have long-term memory and understand:

- User goals
- Investment horizon
- Risk tolerance
- Existing portfolio
- Previous conversations

Suggested prompts:

- Build an RWA portfolio for $10,000
- How should I diversify my investments?
- Compare Treasury RWA and Tokenized Stocks
- What are the safest assets on Mantle?
- How can I generate passive income with RWA?
- Analyze my portfolio risk

The AI should provide explanations and educational context for every
recommendation.

## 3. RWA Academy

A dedicated learning center.

### RWA Fundamentals

- What are Real World Assets?
- Why is tokenization important?

### Investment Basics

- Portfolio diversification
- Risk management
- Compound growth

### Asset Classes

- Treasury products
- Tokenized stocks
- Private credit
- Real estate

### Mantle Ecosystem Guides

- Mantle RWA landscape
- Yield opportunities
- Protocol explanations

All content can be consumed through articles or conversational AI.

## 4. Market Intelligence

A dedicated analytics section.

It should display:

- RWA sector trends
- Capital inflows and outflows
- Historical growth metrics
- Market sentiment
- Important news
- Ecosystem developments

AI-generated summaries should explain:

- What happened?
- Why it happened?
- Potential impact on investors

## 5. Yield Opportunities

A dedicated section focused on income generation.

The platform continuously scans Mantle protocols for opportunities involving
RWA assets.

Examples:

- Lending opportunities
- Vault strategies
- Liquidity pools
- Reward programs
- Leveraged yield strategies

For every opportunity, the platform should eventually display:

- Expected APY
- Risk score
- Lock period
- Strategy description
- Capital efficiency

The AI should explain the advantages and risks before users explore a
strategy.

## 6. Portfolio Planner

Users can create virtual portfolios.

Inputs:

- Capital
- Risk level
- Investment horizon
- Income goals

Outputs:

- Recommended allocation
- Risk score
- Yield projection
- Diversification analysis
- Portfolio explanation

The portfolio should be saveable and trackable over time.

## Core Principle

The platform does not execute trades.

The platform helps users:

- Learn
- Research
- Analyze
- Plan
- Optimize

All decisions remain under user control.
